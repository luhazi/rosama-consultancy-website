About ROSAMA Consultancy

Your Reliable Partner for Business Success in Tanzania

Founded in 2024, ROSAMA Consultancy Ltd has grown into one of Tanzania's most trusted multi-disciplinary consulting firms. We deliver professional tax, accounting, legal compliance, and business advisory services that help organisations stay compliant, reduce risk, and achieve sustainable growth.

ROSAMA was established to fill a critical gap — businesses needed a single firm that could handle every compliance and advisory need. Starting in Dar es Salaam, we expanded our service footprint across Tanzania, earning accreditation from TRA, BRELA, and NBAA. Today we serve over 500 clients across industries including manufacturing, retail, hospitality, NGOs, and international companies.

Our mission is to empower Tanzanian businesses and foreign investors with expert financial and regulatory guidance — enabling them to focus on growth while we handle compliance. We are committed to delivering timely, accurate, and ethical services that build long-term partnerships.

To be Tanzania's most trusted and innovative business consultancy — empowering entrepreneurs and investors to build compliant, sustainable, and prosperous enterprises that contribute to national growth.
